FAB Balance Check

How to Check Your First Abu Dhabi Bank Balance?

Methods to Check FAB Balance

1. Via FAB Mobile App

Download the FAB mobile app, log in, and view your balance on the home screen.

2. Using FAB website

Visit fabbalancecheckae.com, provide the given information and simply check your balance.

3. Checking Balance via ATM

Insert your ATM card, enter your PIN, and select ‘Balance Inquiry’.

4. Using SMS Banking

Send an SMS with "BAL" to the FAB designated number to receive your balance.

5. Calling FAB Customer Service

Dial 600 52 5500 and follow IVR instructions to check your balance.

Final Thoughts

Tracking your FAB account balance is easy through mobile banking, online banking, ATMs, SMS, or customer service.
